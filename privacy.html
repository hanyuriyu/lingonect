Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20 March 2026

1. Overview

Lingonect respects your privacy and is designed to minimise data collection while providing a transparent AI comparison experience.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (used for login and authentication)

User Input

  • Text submitted for translation and comparison

Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Browser and device information
  • Basic usage logs

3. How We Use Data

We use data solely to:

  • Provide translation comparison functionality
  • Display outputs from third-party systems
  • Maintain platform performance and security

We do not use your data for advertising or resale.

4. Third-Party Processing

To generate translation outputs, user input may be sent to third-party providers such as AI and machine translation systems.

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies. Lingonect does not control how these third parties handle data.

5. Data Storage

Lingonect aims to minimise data storage:

  • Translation inputs are not stored longer than necessary to generate results
  • Account data is stored only as required for authentication
  • Technical logs may be retained for security and performance monitoring

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Data is only shared:

  • With third-party providers necessary to deliver the service
  • When required by law

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion
  • Object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@lingonect.com

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no system can be completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact: contact@lingonect.com
